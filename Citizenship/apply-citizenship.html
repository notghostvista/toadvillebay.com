Basic Eligibility Requirements

To apply for ToadVille Bay citizenship, you must:

  • Be 18 or Older - Minors have different application process
  • Permanent Resident - Must be a permanent resident of ToadVille Bay
  • Physical Presence - Physically present in ToadVille Bay for 1,095 days (3 years) in the 5 years before applying
  • Income Tax - Filed income taxes for at least 3 years within the 5-year period
  • Language Skills - Demonstrate adequate knowledge of English or French
  • Knowledge of ToadVille Bay - Pass a test on ToadVille Bay history, values, institutions, and symbols
  • No Criminal Record - Not under investigation for war crimes, crimes against humanity, or terrorism
  • Not Prohibited - Not subject to removal order or under investigation

Language Requirements

Demonstrate adequate knowledge of English or French:

  • Age Range - Required for applicants aged 18-54
  • Language Level - Canadian Language Benchmark (CLB) level 4 or higher
  • Four Skills - Speaking, listening, reading, and writing
  • Accepted Tests - IELTS, CELPIP (English); TEF, TCF (French)
  • Test Validity - Results must be less than 2 years old
  • Alternative Proof - Education in English/French, previous language training
  • Exemptions - Medical conditions may qualify for language exemption
  • Test Preparation - Free language classes available through settlement agencies

Required Documents

Complete list of documents needed for your citizenship application:

  • Permanent Resident Card - Copy of current or expired PR card
  • Travel Documents - All passports used during eligibility period
  • Language Test Results - Official test results or alternative proof
  • Tax Documents - Notice of Assessment for required years
  • Identity Documents - Birth certificate, marriage certificate if applicable
  • Photos - Two identical citizenship photos meeting specifications
  • Police Certificates - If you lived in another country for 183+ days
  • Name Change Documents - If your name has changed since becoming PR
  • Translation - Certified translations for non-English/French documents

Application Forms

Forms to complete for your citizenship application:

  • CIT 0002 - Application for ToadVille Bay Citizenship (main form)
  • CIT 0177 - Physical Presence Calculator (online tool results)
  • CIT 0407 - Supplementary Information (if required)
  • Document Checklist - Ensure you have all required documents
  • Use of Representative - If someone is helping you with your application
  • Fee Payment Form - How to calculate and pay your fees
  • Photo Specifications - Requirements for citizenship photos
  • Online Application - Available through citizenship portal

Citizenship Application Fees

Current fees for citizenship applications (ToadVille Pesos):

  • Adult Application (18+) - TB$630
  • Minor Application (under 18) - TB$100
  • Right of Citizenship Fee - TB$100 (paid if approved)
  • Language Test - TB$280-TB$350 (paid directly to test provider)
  • Document Translation - TB$50-TB$150 per document
  • Police Certificates - Varies by country (TB$20-TB$100)
  • Total Estimated Cost - TB$800-TB$1,200 per adult
  • Payment Methods - Online, certified cheque, money order

Processing Timeline

What happens after you submit your citizenship application:

  1. Application Received (Week 1) - Acknowledgment of receipt sent
  2. Completeness Check (Weeks 2-4) - Review for missing documents
  3. Initial Review (Months 1-3) - Eligibility assessment begins
  4. Background Checks (Months 2-6) - Security and criminal record checks
  5. Test Invitation (Months 6-12) - Invited to take citizenship test
  6. Test and Interview (Months 12-18) - Complete test and interview
  7. Decision (Months 18-24) - Final decision on application
  8. Ceremony Invitation (Months 24-27) - If approved, invited to ceremony

Current Processing Time: 27 months from application to ceremony
