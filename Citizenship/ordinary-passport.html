What is an Ordinary Passport?

The Ordinary Passport is a secure, biometric travel document issued to citizens of ToadVille Bay. It serves as proof of identity and citizenship, allowing holders to travel internationally and access consular services abroad. The Ordinary Passport contains the holder's name, photograph, date of birth, and other identifying information. It is an essential document for international travel and is required for entry into most countries around the world.
