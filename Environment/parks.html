Waterfront Park

Our flagship park offers stunning views and diverse recreational opportunities:

  • Promenade - 2km paved walkway along the shoreline
  • Beach Access - Sandy beach with swimming area (seasonal lifeguards)
  • Picnic Areas - Multiple pavilions and BBQ facilities
  • Playground - Modern equipment for ages 2-12
  • Boat Launch - Public ramp for small watercraft
  • Parking - 200 spaces including accessible spots

Forest Grove Park

Immerse yourself in nature at our largest green space:

  • Old-Growth Forest - Protected 50-hectare forest preserve
  • Nature Center - Educational programs and exhibits
  • Hiking Trails - 8km of marked trails for all skill levels
  • Wildlife Viewing - Bird watching platforms and quiet zones
  • Adventure Playground - Natural play structures
  • Group Camping - Overnight facilities for organized groups

Community Gardens

Grow your own food and connect with fellow gardeners:

  • Plot Rental - Seasonal plots available for residents
  • Shared Tools - Common tool shed and water access
  • Workshops - Monthly gardening education sessions
  • Composting - Community composting program
  • Children's Garden - Special area for young gardeners
  • Harvest Sharing - Donate surplus to local food bank
