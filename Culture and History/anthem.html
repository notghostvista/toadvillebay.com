National Anthem of ToadVille Bay

The official anthem of ToadVille Bay, presented in multiple languages.

About the Anthem

The national anthem of ToadVille Bay was created in 1945 and was officially enacted as the national anthem in 1952.

The National anthem should be played during events, if on ToadVille Soil, the ToadVille national anthem shall be played first, and at end of an event, shall be played last, there should be no events of clapping once the anthem has concluded
It should be noted that the ToadVille Bay National anthem is hevaily based off the National Anthem of Canada, if not, mostly based, please refer to that.
