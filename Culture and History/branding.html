What This Page Is

This page contains official ToadVille Bay branding assets and cultural icons. Use these for web, print, media, and community projects when referencing ToadVille Bay.

What Each Asset Is Used For

  • ToadVille Bay Wordmark (Global Branding): The official logotype used for global identity and consistent branding across platforms (web, print, media).
  • TB Coat of Arms, National Flag, and Gabe the Narwhal (Cultural Icons): Cultural and national symbols used to represent heritage, identity, and community storytelling.
