Media & Entertainment

GhostVista Gets Grounded

GhostVista Gets Grounded is a YouTube series set within the cultural sphere of ToadVille Bay. The show follows a kid who repeatedly causes trouble and faces the consequences of his actions.

Rated TV-14, the series is intended for a teen audience and uses exaggerated humor and discipline-based storytelling.

  • Category: Media & Entertainment
  • Status: Not an official government or national symbol
  • Cultural role: Part of modern ToadVille Bay internet culture
