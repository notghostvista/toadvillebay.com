Branding

Official visual identity assets for ToadVille Bay — for web, print, and community use.

Overview

This page contains official ToadVille Bay branding assets, including the national flag, the Coat of Arms, and official wordmarks. Use these when referencing ToadVille Bay in websites, documents, media, events, and community projects.

Official Branding Assets

Brand Use Guidelines

  • Don’t distort: Avoid stretching, squishing, or skewing any asset.
  • Keep it readable: Leave space around the flag, Coat of Arms, and wordmarks so they don’t feel crowded.
  • Respect context: Don’t use the branding to imply official approval of unofficial groups, products, or events.
  • Stay consistent: Use the English or French wordmark depending on language context (or both when appropriate).
