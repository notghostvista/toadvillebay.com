1964 Texica Province Independence Referendum

This page display the referendum that had taken place in the Texica province asking it's citizens wether their province should become an independent nation or stay within the Republic of ToadVille Bay.

Question of the Referendum

English: Do you agree that Texica should become sovereign after recent polls showing the desire for self governance?

Español: ¿Está de acuerdo en que Texica debería volverse soberana tras recientes encuestas que muestran el deseo de autogobernarse?

Français: Acceptez-vous que Texica devienne souveraine après de récents sondages montrant un désir d’autogouvernance ?

ToadVille: ¿ Acceptüz-vox qou Texica deberïoz soverenïskio ûegt pöllis regremento setrï l'desroix güpe govbierno-selfex ?

Post-referendum

Independence: To avoid any conflicts, the government of the Republic of ToadVille Bay had allowed the declaration of independence of the Republic of Texica (Socialist Federation of Texica) to pass within their parliament on 20 August 1964, granting them independence while keeping an eye out on their government.
