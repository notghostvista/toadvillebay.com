2004 Parliamentary Transformation Referendum

Held on May 28, 2004, this referendum asked citizens to approve the transformation of ToadVille Bay into a parliamentary republic.

⚠ This is a historical record. The referendum passed with overwhelming support.
🗳️

Referendum Details

Question and results from the 2004 Parliamentary Transformation Referendum.

Question of the Referendum

Do you approve that the Republic of ToadVille Bay be transformed into a parliamentary republic, meaning that instead of electing a President as Head of State, you elect a Prime Minister as Head of Government; that, if approved, the Government of ToadVille Bay will begin a gradual transition to such a parliamentary system, appoint an interim Prime Minister until the first permanent Prime Minister is elected, create a House of Commons, and establish electoral districts for parliamentary representation?
