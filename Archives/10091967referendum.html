1967 Independence Referendum

Held in 1967, this referendum asked citizens whether ToadVille Island should declare independence unilaterally.

⚠ Historical record. This referendum marked a major turning point in ToadVille Island governance.
🗳️

Referendum Details

Population: 953,930 • Turnout: 924,294

Question of the Referendum

Should ToadVille Island declare independence unilaterally?
