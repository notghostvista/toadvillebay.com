Understanding Your Application Number

Your application number format and where to find it:

  • Format - Usually starts with letters followed by 9-10 digits (e.g., WP2025123456789)
  • Receipt Notice - Found on your application receipt/acknowledgment letter
  • Email Confirmation - Included in your submission confirmation email
  • Case Sensitive - Enter exactly as shown, including letters and numbers
  • Multiple Applications - Each family member has a separate number
  • Keep It Safe - Treat like a password; don't share publicly
  • Lost Your Number? - Contact our support center for assistance

Required Personal Information

Information needed to access your application status:

  • Date of Birth - Must match exactly what's on your application
  • Country of Birth - As listed on your passport or birth certificate
  • Last Name - Family name as shown on your application
  • Given Names - First and middle names from your application
  • Passport Number - Current passport used for the application
  • Security Questions - May be asked additional verification questions
  • Name Changes - Use the name from your original application

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Solutions for common status checking problems:

  • Application Not Found - Check application number spelling and format
  • Information Mismatch - Ensure personal details match your application exactly
  • System Maintenance - Portal may be unavailable during scheduled maintenance
  • Browser Issues - Clear cache and cookies, try different browser
  • Account Locked - Too many failed attempts; wait 24 hours or contact support
  • Old Applications - Applications older than 2 years may not be in the system
  • Technical Support - Call (250) 555-STATUS for technical help
  • Office Hours - Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM Pacific Time

Understanding Status Messages

  • "Application Received" - We have received your complete application
  • "In Process" - Your application is being reviewed by an officer
  • "Additional Documents Required" - We need more information from you
  • "Background Check in Progress" - Security and criminal checks underway
  • "Medical Exam Required" - You need to complete medical examination
  • "Interview Scheduled" - You have been scheduled for an interview
  • "Decision Made" - A decision has been made on your application
  • "Application Approved" - Your application has been approved
  • "Application Refused" - Your application has been refused
