Rights and responsibilities

  • Live, work, and study anywhere in ToadVille Bay.
  • Access most social benefits and protections under the law.
  • Maintain residency obligation (e.g., minimum physical presence over a period).
  • Pay taxes and respect local, regional, and national laws.

Travel and your status

Your PR Card is your proof of status for re-entering the country on commercial transport. Keep it valid and carry it when you travel.
