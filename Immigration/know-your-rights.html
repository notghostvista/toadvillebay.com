Basic Rights

Right to Remain Silent

  • You have the right to remain silent
  • You can refuse to answer questions
  • You can request an attorney
  • Show your valid immigration documents if requested

Right to an Attorney

  • Right to legal representation
  • Can request a lawyer at any time
  • Free legal services may be available
  • Attorney-client privilege protection

Due Process Rights

  • Right to a fair hearing
  • Right to present evidence
  • Right to appeal decisions
  • Right to an interpreter

Workplace Rights

Fair Treatment

  • Protection from discrimination
  • Equal pay for equal work
  • Safe working conditions
  • Right to join unions

Wage Protection

  • Minimum wage guarantee
  • Overtime compensation
  • Right to report violations
  • Protection from retaliation

Work Documentation

  • Right to maintain copies
  • Access to personnel files
  • Privacy of information
  • Documentation verification

Law Enforcement Encounters

At Your Home

  • Right to see a warrant
  • Can refuse entry without warrant
  • Right to speak through door
  • Can request interpreter

In Public Places

  • Right to walk away
  • Record interactions legally
  • Ask if you're free to go
  • Maintain calm demeanor

During Detention

  • Right to remain silent
  • Right to contact attorney
  • No forced document signing
  • Medical care if needed
