Spouse & Partner Sponsorship

Sponsor your spouse, common-law partner, or conjugal partner for permanent residence:

  • Spouse - Legally married to you
  • Common-Law Partner - Living together for at least 12 months
  • Conjugal Partner - In relationship for at least 12 months but unable to live together
  • Processing Time - 12-15 months for most applications
  • Fee - TB$1,080 (sponsorship fee + principal applicant fee)
  • Medical Exam - Required for sponsored person

Dependent Children

Sponsor your dependent children for permanent residence:

  • Age Requirement - Under 22 years old and unmarried
  • Over 22 - Eligible if financially dependent due to mental/physical condition
  • Biological or Adopted - Both biological and legally adopted children eligible
  • Processing Time - 8-12 months when included with spouse application
  • Fee - TB$150 per child
  • Documentation - Birth certificates, adoption papers if applicable

Parents & Grandparents Program

Annual program to sponsor parents and grandparents:

  • Annual Intake - Limited number of applications accepted each year
  • Interest to Sponsor - Must submit form during intake period
  • Income Requirements - Must meet minimum income for 3 consecutive years
  • Undertaking - 20-year commitment to provide financial support
  • Processing Time - 20-24 months after invitation to apply
  • Fee - TB$1,050 per parent/grandparent

Sponsor Eligibility Requirements

To sponsor a family member, you must:

  • Status - Be a ToadVille Bay citizen or permanent resident
  • Age - Be at least 18 years old
  • Financial - Meet minimum income requirements and sign undertaking
  • Criminal Record - No serious criminal convictions
  • Previous Sponsorships - Must have fulfilled any previous undertakings
  • Bankruptcy - Not be in default of court-ordered support payments

Application Process

Steps to complete your family sponsorship application:

  1. Check Eligibility - Ensure you and your family member meet requirements
  2. Gather Documents - Collect all required forms and supporting documents
  3. Pay Fees - Submit application fees and biometrics fees
  4. Submit Application - Send complete application package
  5. Biometrics - Sponsored person provides fingerprints and photo
  6. Medical Exam - Sponsored person completes medical examination
  7. Interview - May be required for some applications
  8. Decision - Receive final decision and landing documents
