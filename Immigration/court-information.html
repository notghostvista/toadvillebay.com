Important Notice

Court Attendance is Mandatory: Failure to appear at your scheduled hearing may result in:

  • Order of removal in absentia
  • Loss of relief eligibility
  • Arrest warrant issuance
  • Immediate deportation order

If you cannot attend your hearing, contact the court immediately at 555-0150.
