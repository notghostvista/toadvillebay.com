Employer-Specific Work Permits

Most work permits are tied to a specific employer and job:

  • LMIA Required - Employer must prove no ToadVille Bay worker available
  • Job Offer - Must have written job offer before applying
  • Specific Conditions - Can only work for named employer
  • Duration - Usually 1-4 years, based on job offer
  • Renewal - Can renew if job continues and LMIA valid
  • Fee - TB$155 work permit fee + TB$100 open work permit holder fee

Open Work Permits

Work for any employer without needing LMIA (limited eligibility):

  • Spouse of Worker/Student - If partner has valid permit
  • International Students - Post-graduation work permit
  • Refugee Claimants - 150 days after claim filed
  • Vulnerable Workers - Escaping abuse from employer
  • International Mobility Program - Specific agreements
  • Fee - TB$255 (includes open work permit holder fee)

Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)

Most employers need LMIA before hiring foreign workers:

  • Employer Responsibility - Employer applies and pays for LMIA
  • Job Advertisement - Must advertise job to ToadVille Bay workers first
  • Wage Requirements - Must pay prevailing wage or higher
  • Processing Time - 2-6 months for LMIA decision
  • LMIA Fee - TB$1,000 per position (paid by employer)
  • Exemptions - Some jobs don't need LMIA (NAFTA, etc.)

Eligibility Requirements

To get a work permit, you must:

  • Job Offer - Have valid job offer from ToadVille Bay employer
  • Qualifications - Meet education and experience requirements
  • Language Skills - Demonstrate required language ability
  • Medical Exam - Pass medical exam if required
  • Criminal Record - Provide police certificates if requested
  • Financial Support - Show you can support yourself
  • Temporary Stay - Convince officer you'll leave when permit expires

Required Documents

Documents needed for your work permit application:

  • Application Forms - Completed and signed application package
  • Job Offer Letter - Detailed offer including salary and conditions
  • LMIA - Positive LMIA from employer (if required)
  • Passport - Valid passport and travel documents
  • Photos - Recent passport-style photographs
  • Education Documents - Diplomas, degrees, transcripts
  • Work Experience - Letters from previous employers
  • Language Test - Proof of language ability (if required)
