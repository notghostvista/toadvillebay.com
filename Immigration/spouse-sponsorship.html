Spouse Sponsorship

Sponsor your legally married spouse for permanent residence:

  • Legal Marriage - Must be legally married under ToadVille Bay or foreign law
  • Marriage Certificate - Provide official marriage certificate with translation
  • Proxy/Telephone Marriages - Generally not accepted unless consummated
  • Same-Sex Marriage - Fully recognized and eligible for sponsorship
  • Multiple Marriages - Only one spouse can be sponsored at a time
  • Previous Divorces - Must provide divorce certificates from previous marriages

Common-Law Partner Sponsorship

Sponsor your common-law partner with whom you've lived for 12+ months:

  • 12-Month Requirement - Must have lived together continuously for at least 12 months
  • Marriage-Like Relationship - Committed relationship similar to marriage
  • Proof of Cohabitation - Joint bank accounts, lease agreements, bills
  • Temporary Separations - Brief separations for work/family don't break continuity
  • Social Recognition - Present yourselves as a couple to family/friends
  • Financial Interdependence - Shared financial responsibilities and support

Conjugal Partner Sponsorship

For partners in committed relationships who cannot live together or marry:

  • Exceptional Circumstances - Immigration barriers, legal restrictions, etc.
  • 12-Month Relationship - In relationship for at least 12 months
  • Cannot Marry - Legal, religious, or social barriers to marriage
  • Cannot Live Together - Immigration status prevents cohabitation
  • Significant Degree of Attachment - Similar to spouse/common-law relationship
  • Higher Evidence Standard - More documentation required to prove relationship

Sponsor Eligibility Requirements

To sponsor your spouse or partner, you must:

  • Be 18 or Older - Must be at least 18 years of age
  • ToadVille Bay Status - Be a citizen or permanent resident
  • Live in ToadVille Bay - Or plan to return when your partner becomes a PR
  • Financial Requirements - Meet minimum income and sign undertaking
  • No Criminal Record - No serious criminal convictions
  • Not in Default - No outstanding immigration loans or support payments
  • Previous Sponsorships - Must have fulfilled any previous undertakings
  • Not Receiving Social Assistance - Except for disability benefits

Application Steps

Complete process for sponsoring your spouse or partner:

  1. Check Eligibility - Ensure you and your partner meet all requirements
  2. Gather Documents - Collect relationship proof, identity documents, forms
  3. Complete Forms - Fill out sponsorship and permanent residence applications
  4. Pay Fees - Submit TB$1,080 in government fees
  5. Submit Application - Send complete package to processing center
  6. Acknowledgment - Receive confirmation and application number
  7. Biometrics - Sponsored person provides fingerprints and photo
  8. Medical Exam - Sponsored person completes medical examination
  9. Background Checks - Police certificates and security screening
  10. Interview - May be required to verify relationship authenticity
  11. Decision - Receive final decision and landing documents

Processing Timeline & What to Expect

Understanding the process after you apply:

  • Processing Time - 12-15 months for most applications
  • Application Review - Initial completeness check (30-60 days)
  • Eligibility Assessment - Review of sponsor and applicant eligibility
  • Relationship Assessment - Verification of genuine relationship
  • Background Checks - Security, criminality, and medical clearances
  • Interview - Required if relationship authenticity questioned
  • Decision - Approval, refusal, or request for additional information
  • Landing - Final step to become permanent resident

Fees & Costs

  • Sponsorship Fee - TB$75 (sponsor application)
  • Principal Applicant Fee - TB$475 (sponsored person)
  • Right of Permanent Residence Fee - TB$500 (if approved)
  • Biometrics Fee - TB$85 per person
  • Medical Exam - TB$200-TB$400 (varies by country)
  • Police Certificates - Varies by country (TB$20-TB$100)
  • Translation Costs - TB$50-TB$150 per document
  • Total Estimated Cost - TB$1,400-TB$1,800

Tips for a Strong Application

  • Document Everything - Provide extensive proof of your relationship
  • Tell Your Story - Include detailed relationship history and timeline
  • Include Photos - Pictures together at different times and places
  • Third-Party Evidence - Letters from family and friends who know your relationship
  • Financial Records - Joint accounts, shared expenses, insurance beneficiaries
  • Communication Records - Phone logs, emails, messages during separations
  • Travel Records - Visits to each other's countries
  • Be Honest - Disclose any previous relationships, marriages, or issues
