Important Notice

Beware of Unauthorized Representatives: Only work with licensed lawyers or registered immigration consultants. Unauthorized representatives may:

  • Provide incorrect advice
  • Charge excessive fees
  • Submit incorrect documentation
  • Damage your immigration case

Report unauthorized representatives to the Immigration Department at 555-0130.
