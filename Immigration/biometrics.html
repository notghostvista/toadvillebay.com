ToadVille Bay Biometrics Center

Main biometrics collection site serving ToadVille Bay and surrounding areas:

  • Address - 456 Immigration Boulevard, ToadVille Bay, TB V1A 2B3
  • Hours - Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Closed - Sundays and statutory holidays
  • Parking - Free 2-hour parking available on-site
  • Public Transit - Routes 1, 2, and 4 stop within 2 blocks
  • Accessibility - Fully wheelchair accessible with elevator access
  • Languages - Service available in English, French, Spanish, and Mandarin
  • Appointment Required - No walk-ins accepted at this location

Mobile Biometrics Services

Traveling biometrics collection for remote and underserved areas:

  • Schedule - Visits remote communities on rotating monthly schedule
  • Advance Booking Required - Must book at least 2 weeks in advance
  • Minimum Group Size - 10 people required for mobile service visit
  • Community Coordination - Work with local community leaders to organize
  • Equipment - Full biometrics collection capability in mobile unit
  • Cost - Same TB$85 fee as regular locations
  • Weather Dependent - Service may be cancelled due to severe weather
  • Contact - Call (250) 555-MOBILE to request mobile service

What to Bring to Your Appointment

Required documents and items for your biometrics appointment:

  • Biometrics Instruction Letter - The letter you received with your application number
  • Valid Passport - Current passport used for your immigration application
  • Photo Identification - Government-issued ID with your photo
  • Application Receipt - Proof of payment for biometrics fee (TB$85)
  • Appointment Confirmation - Email or printed confirmation of your appointment
  • Glasses - If you normally wear glasses, bring them for the photo
  • Contact Lenses - Colored contacts must be removed before photo
  • Children's Documents - Birth certificate for children under 14

Tips for a Smooth Appointment

Make your biometrics appointment quick and easy:

  • Arrive Early - Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time
  • Clean Hands - Wash hands thoroughly before fingerprinting
  • Remove Jewelry - Take off rings and bracelets that might interfere
  • Avoid Hand Lotion - Don't use lotion on hands day of appointment
  • Dress Appropriately - Avoid clothing that covers your face or head
  • Bring Documents - Have all required documents organized and ready
  • Stay Calm - Relax during the process; staff will guide you
  • Ask Questions - Don't hesitate to ask if you need clarification

Accessibility Services

Accommodations available for people with disabilities:

  • Wheelchair Access - All locations are wheelchair accessible
  • Sign Language - ASL interpreters available with advance notice
  • Visual Impairment - Staff assistance available for visually impaired clients
  • Hearing Impairment - Written instructions and visual cues provided
  • Mobility Assistance - Staff can help with positioning for biometrics
  • Service Animals - Service animals welcome at all locations
  • Extended Time - Additional time allocated for clients who need it
  • Request Accommodations - Call ahead to arrange special accommodations

What Happens During Your Appointment

  • Check-In - Present your documents at the reception desk
  • Verification - Staff will verify your identity and application
  • Digital Photo - Professional digital photograph taken
  • Fingerprints - Electronic fingerprint scanning (all 10 fingers)
  • Quality Check - Staff ensure all biometrics meet standards
  • Completion - Receive confirmation that biometrics are complete
  • Duration - Entire process typically takes 15-30 minutes
  • Next Steps - Information about what happens next in your application
