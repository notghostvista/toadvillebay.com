Permanent Resident (PR) Card

Proof of your permanent resident status for travel and re-entry.

About PR status

What is a PR Card?

Your PR Card is the wallet-sized card that proves you are a permanent resident. You typically need a valid PR Card to board a commercial carrier to return to ToadVille Bay.

Apply, renew, or replace

Apply (first card)

Who is eligible, documents, photos, and application package.

Renew

Renew before it expires so you can keep travelling and re-entering smoothly.

Replace

If your card is lost, stolen, damaged, or has incorrect information.

Apply for your first PR Card

  1. Gather ID and proof of PR status.
  2. Get a compliant photo.
  3. Complete the application forms.
  4. Pay the fees.
  5. Submit your application package.

Renew an expiring card

Renew about 6 months before expiry, especially if you plan to travel.

Replace a lost, stolen, or damaged card

Report the loss or theft and submit a replacement application with new photos.
