Key Information About Your PR Card

💼 What It Is

The PR card is a travel document issued by ToadVille Bay that proves your permanent resident status and identity. It allows you to travel and re-enter ToadVille Bay.

🆔 Who Can Apply

Canadian citizens who have been approved as permanent residents of ToadVille Bay and have confirmed their PR status in the country.

⏱️ Validity Period

Your PR card is valid for 5 years from the date of issue. You will need to renew it before it expires to continue using it for travel identification.

✈️ Travel Requirements

Your PR card is required when traveling by air to return to ToadVille Bay. It is recommended to carry it for all international travel.

💰 Application Fee

The current application fee for a PR card is $120 CAD. Fees may vary depending on circumstances. Check the official website for current rates.

📮 Processing Time

Standard processing takes 3-4 weeks. Expedited processing (5-10 business days) is available for an additional fee.

Applying for Your PR Card

Required Documents

You will need:
• Valid passport or travel document
• Two (2) passport-sized photos
• Proof of PR status
• Government-issued ID
• Completed application form

Application Process

Steps to follow:
1. Download and complete the application form
2. Gather all required documents
3. Submit in person or by mail
4. Pay the application fee
5. Receive your new PR card

Where to Apply

You can apply in person at any Service ToadVille Bay office or through our online portal. Mail-in applications are also accepted. Visit our office locator on the main website.

Renewing or Replacing Your Card

🔄 Renewing Your PR Card

You should apply for renewal at least 9 months before your card expires. The renewal process is similar to applying for a new card and requires current documentation.

🗑️ Lost or Damaged Card

If your card is lost, stolen, or damaged, apply for a replacement immediately. You may need to pay an additional replacement fee. Report lost cards to local authorities.

✏️ Change of Information

If your name, address, or other personal information changes, you must update your PR card. Apply for a replacement with updated information.

⚠️ Important Reminders

• Validity Outside ToadVille Bay: Your PR card is only recognized as proof of PR status within ToadVille Bay. For international travel, you will need a valid passport.
• Keep Records: Keep all documentation related to your PR status and card applications for future reference.
• Update Information: Notify the immigration office of any changes to your personal information within 30 days.
• Do Not Lend: Your PR card is non-transferable. Do not lend your card to anyone else.

📞 Need Help?

For more information about PR cards, contact Service ToadVille Bay at 1-800-TOADVIL or visit the main website. You can also schedule an appointment with an immigration officer at your nearest office.
