Tourist Visa

Visit ToadVille Bay for tourism, family visits, or personal reasons:

  • Duration - Up to 6 months per visit
  • Multiple Entry - Valid for 10 years or until passport expires
  • Processing Time - 2-4 weeks for most applications
  • Fee - TB$100 per person, TB$500 for families (5+ people)
  • Requirements - Valid passport, proof of funds, return ticket
  • Extensions - Possible in exceptional circumstances

Business Visitor Visa

For business activities that don't involve working for a ToadVille Bay employer:

  • Permitted Activities - Meetings, conferences, trade shows, negotiations
  • Duration - Up to 6 months, multiple entries allowed
  • Fast Track - 5-day processing for established business travelers
  • Fee - TB$150 per person
  • Documentation - Business invitation, company registration, itinerary
  • Restrictions - Cannot engage in direct sales to consumers

Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)

Quick online authorization for eligible travelers from visa-exempt countries:

  • Eligible Countries - 45+ countries with visa exemption agreements
  • Application - Online form takes 10-15 minutes
  • Processing - Most applications approved within hours
  • Fee - TB$7 per application
  • Validity - 5 years or until passport expires
  • Purpose - Tourism, business visits, transit only

Work Permits

Authorization to work temporarily in ToadVille Bay:

  • Employer-Specific - Tied to specific employer and position
  • Open Work Permit - Work for any employer (limited eligibility)
  • Duration - Usually 1-4 years, renewable
  • LMIA Required - Most positions need Labour Market Impact Assessment
  • Processing Time - 8-16 weeks depending on country of residence
  • Fee - TB$155 work permit + TB$100 open work permit holder fee

Study Permits

For international students attending designated learning institutions:

  • Letter of Acceptance - Required from designated institution
  • Financial Support - Proof of funds for tuition and living expenses
  • Duration - Length of study program plus 90 days
  • Work Authorization - 20 hours/week during studies, full-time during breaks
  • Processing Time - 4-12 weeks depending on country
  • Fee - TB$150 per application
