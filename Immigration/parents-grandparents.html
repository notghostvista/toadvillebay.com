Parents & Grandparents Program (PGP)

Annual program for permanent residence sponsorship:

  • Annual Intake - Limited to 28,500 applications per year
  • Interest to Sponsor - Must submit form during intake period (usually October)
  • Random Selection - Invitations issued by lottery from interest pool
  • 60-Day Response - Must submit complete application within 60 days of invitation
  • Processing Time - 20-24 months after invitation to apply
  • Fee - TB$1,050 per parent/grandparent + TB$500 right of PR fee
  • Medical Exam - Required for all applicants
  • No Appeals - Refused applications cannot be appealed

Super Visa

Multi-entry visa allowing extended stays with children/grandchildren:

  • Validity - Up to 10 years (or passport expiry)
  • Stay Duration - Up to 5 years per visit
  • Multiple Entry - Can leave and return multiple times
  • Insurance Required - TB$100,000 emergency medical coverage
  • Income Requirement - Child/grandchild must meet minimum income
  • Processing Time - 2-4 months
  • Fee - TB$100 per person
  • Medical Exam - Required for applicants from certain countries

Regular Visitor Visa

Standard visitor visa for parents and grandparents:

  • Stay Duration - Up to 6 months per visit
  • Validity - Usually 10 years or until passport expires
  • Lower Requirements - Less stringent than Super Visa
  • No Insurance Requirement - Not mandatory but recommended
  • Processing Time - 2-8 weeks depending on country
  • Fee - TB$100 per person
  • Extensions Possible - Can apply to extend stay while in ToadVille Bay

Income Requirements

Minimum income thresholds for sponsoring parents and grandparents:

  • 3-Year Requirement - Must meet income for 3 consecutive taxation years
  • Family Size Calculation - Includes sponsor, spouse, dependent children, and sponsored persons
  • 2023 Income Thresholds (TB$):
    • 2 persons: TB$33,141
    • 3 persons: TB$40,742
    • 4 persons: TB$49,466
    • 5 persons: TB$56,104
    • 6 persons: TB$63,276
    • 7+ persons: Add TB$7,172 for each additional person
  • Income Sources - Employment, self-employment, pension, investments
  • Not Counted - Social assistance, employment insurance, child benefits
  • Co-Signer Allowed - Spouse/partner can combine income

Application Process

Complete process for Parents & Grandparents Program:

  1. Check Eligibility - Ensure you meet all sponsor requirements
  2. Submit Interest to Sponsor - During annual intake period
  3. Wait for Invitation - Random selection from interest pool
  4. Gather Documents - Collect all required forms and supporting documents
  5. Complete Applications - Sponsorship and permanent residence applications
  6. Pay Fees - Government processing fees
  7. Submit Complete Package - Within 60 days of invitation
  8. Acknowledgment - Receive confirmation and file number
  9. Medical Exams - Parents/grandparents complete medical examination
  10. Background Checks - Police certificates and security screening
  11. Interview - May be required in some cases
  12. Decision - Approval or refusal notification
  13. Landing - Final step to become permanent residents

Undertaking Obligations

20-year commitment when sponsoring parents and grandparents:

  • Duration - 20 years from the date they become permanent residents
  • Financial Support - Provide for basic needs (food, clothing, shelter)
  • Healthcare Costs - Cover healthcare costs not covered by public system
  • Social Assistance - Repay any social assistance they receive
  • Joint Responsibility - Spouse/partner also bound by undertaking
  • Continues After Death - Undertaking continues even if sponsor dies
  • Continues After Divorce - Undertaking continues even if sponsor divorces
  • Legal Obligation - Enforceable by law; government can sue for repayment

Alternative Options

If you don't qualify for PGP or want faster options:

  • Super Visa - Faster processing, allows long stays
  • Multiple Entry Visitor Visa - For regular shorter visits
  • Provincial Nominee Programs - Some provinces have parent sponsorship streams
  • Caregiver Programs - If parent can work as caregiver
  • Humanitarian & Compassionate - Exceptional circumstances
  • Wait for Next Year - Resubmit interest to sponsor form annually

Application Tips

  • Submit Interest Early - Submit on first day of intake period
  • Keep Documents Ready - Have all documents prepared before invitation
  • Meet Income Requirements - Ensure 3 years of qualifying income
  • Complete Application - Submit 100% complete package to avoid delays
  • Consider Super Visa - Apply for Super Visa while waiting for PGP
  • Get Professional Help - Consider immigration consultant for complex cases
