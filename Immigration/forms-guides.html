Current Immigration Fees (ToadVille Pesos)

Up-to-date fee schedule for all immigration services:

  • Work Permit - TB$155
  • Study Permit - TB$150
  • Visitor Visa - TB$100
  • Super Visa - TB$100
  • eTA - TB$7
  • Biometrics - TB$85 per person
  • Open Work Permit Holder Fee - TB$100
  • Spouse Sponsorship - TB$1,080 total
  • Parents & Grandparents Sponsorship - TB$1,050 per person
  • Right of Permanent Residence Fee - TB$500
  • PR Card (new/renewal/replacement) - TB$50
  • Citizenship Application - TB$630 adult, TB$100 minor

Accepted Payment Methods

How to pay your immigration fees:

  • Online Payment - Credit card, debit card, or online banking
  • Bank Draft - Certified cheque or bank draft in ToadVille Pesos
  • Money Order - Postal money order or certified money order
  • Credit Cards Accepted - Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • Payment Receipt - Must include receipt with your application
  • Currency - All payments must be in ToadVille Pesos (TB$)
  • Processing Fee - Additional TB$2.50 for online credit card payments
  • Refund Policy - Fees are generally non-refundable

Fee Exemptions & Reductions

Who qualifies for fee reductions or exemptions:

  • Financial Hardship - May qualify for fee exemption with documentation
  • Protected Persons - Refugees and protected persons may be exempt
  • Victims of Human Trafficking - Special fee exemptions available
  • Dependent Children - Reduced fees for children under 22
  • Family Applications - Reduced fees when applying as a family
  • Correction Applications - No fee for correcting government errors
  • How to Apply - Submit request with supporting documentation
  • Processing Time - Fee exemption requests add 4-6 weeks
