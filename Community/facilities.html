Community Center

Our main community facility offers versatile spaces for all types of events:

  • Main Hall - Capacity 200 people, stage, sound system
  • Meeting Rooms - 3 rooms accommodating 10-30 people each
  • Commercial Kitchen - Full catering facilities available
  • Outdoor Patio - Connected space for receptions
  • Accessibility - Fully accessible with elevator and ramps

Sports Complex

Modern athletic facilities for sports, fitness, and recreation:

  • Gymnasium - Full court with bleacher seating
  • Fitness Studio - Mirrored room for classes and training
  • Outdoor Courts - Tennis, basketball, and pickleball
  • Equipment Storage - Available for organized events
  • Locker Rooms - Changing facilities and showers

Park Pavilions

Beautiful outdoor venues in our community parks:

  • Waterfront Pavilion - Scenic bay views, capacity 100
  • Forest Grove Shelter - Wooded setting, capacity 50
  • Playground Pavilion - Near children's play area
  • Picnic Areas - Multiple tables and BBQ facilities
  • Parking - Ample parking at all locations

Rental Rates

Affordable rates with discounts for residents and non-profits:

  • Residents - 50% discount on all facility rentals
  • Non-profits - 25% discount with proof of status
  • Damage deposit - Required for all bookings, fully refundable
  • Cleaning fee - Optional service or clean yourself
