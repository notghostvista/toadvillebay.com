About Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Enable certain site functionality
  • Help us understand how you use our services

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

  • Security features
  • Basic functionality
  • Session management
  • Load balancing

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences.

  • Language preferences
  • Region settings
  • Accessibility options
  • User interface customization

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website.

  • Visit duration
  • Pages visited
  • Traffic sources
  • User behavior

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site, and some services and functionalities may not work.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View cookies stored on your computer
  • Allow, block, or delete cookies
  • Set cookie preferences for different websites
  • Configure third-party cookie settings
