How Expansion Works

In expansion regions, the ToadVille Bay platform may be localized to better match language and cultural context. Localized versions can include exclusive, region-specific content that may not be available elsewhere.

Platform Localization

  • Localized language and regional formatting
  • Cultural content tailored to the region
  • Exclusive content or features for that locale
