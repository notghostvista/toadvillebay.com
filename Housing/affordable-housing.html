Rental Assistance Program

Help with rent payments for eligible households:

  • Eligibility - Household income below 50% of area median income
  • Assistance Amount - Up to 70% of rent, maximum TB$800/month
  • Duration - Renewable annually with income verification
  • Application - Apply online or at Community Services office
  • Waiting List - Priority given to families with children and seniors
  • Requirements - Must be Canadian citizen or permanent resident

Social Housing Units

Community-owned affordable rental housing:

  • Bayview Apartments - 48 units for families, rent geared to income
  • Seniors Manor - 32 one-bedroom units for adults 55+
  • Transitional Housing - 12 units for individuals moving to independence
  • Rent Calculation - 30% of household income maximum
  • Utilities Included - Heat, water, and basic cable included
  • Maintenance - 24/7 emergency maintenance service

First-Time Homebuyer Program

Support for purchasing your first home:

  • Down Payment Loan - Up to TB$25,000 interest-free for 5 years
  • Education Workshops - Free courses on homebuying process
  • Credit Counseling - Help improving credit scores and debt management
  • Income Limits - Household income below 80% of area median
  • Property Limits - Purchase price under TB$400,000
  • Occupancy Requirement - Must live in home as primary residence
