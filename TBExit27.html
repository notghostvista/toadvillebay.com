What is an eTA?

An Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) is an entry requirement for visa-exempt foreign nationals traveling to ToadVille Bay by air. It's electronically linked to your passport and is valid for up to five years or until your passport expires, whichever comes first.

  • No Documents Required: Just your passport and basic information

How to Apply

Citizens from the newly added countries can apply for an eTA online at eta.toadvillebay.ca. The application process is simple and straightforward:

  1. Complete the online form ‚Äì Takes about 5-10 minutes
  2. Pay the TB$7 fee ‚Äì Credit card or debit card accepted
  3. Receive approval ‚Äì Usually within minutes via email
  4. Travel to ToadVille Bay ‚Äì Your eTA is electronically linked to your passport

Important Requirements

To be eligible for an eTA, travelers must:

  • Hold a valid passport from one of the eligible countries
  • Be traveling to ToadVille Bay by air
  • Have no criminal record or immigration violations
  • Be in good health
  • Have sufficient funds for their stay
  • Intend to leave ToadVille Bay at the end of their visit

"This expansion will significantly boost tourism and business connections between ToadVille Bay and these five countries," said Minister of Immigration Sarah Toadsworth. "We expect to see increased visitor numbers, which will benefit our tourism industry and strengthen international partnerships."

Transition Period

Citizens from these countries who currently hold valid visitor visas can continue to use them until they expire. However, they may choose to apply for an eTA for future travel, which offers greater convenience and cost savings.
