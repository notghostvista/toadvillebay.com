Local Bus Service

Our comprehensive local network serves all areas of ToadVille Bay:

  • Route 1 - Downtown to Waterfront (every 15 minutes)
  • Route 2 - Residential loop via Community Center (every 20 minutes)
  • Route 3 - Industrial area to Shopping District (every 30 minutes)
  • Route 4 - Express to Regional Hospital (hourly)
  • Weekend Service - Reduced frequency on Routes 1 & 2
  • Holiday Schedule - Limited service on statutory holidays

Fares & Passes

Affordable transportation for everyone:

  • Adult Single Ride - TB$2.50 (exact change or transit card)
  • Youth/Senior - TB$1.75 (ages 13-18 and 65+)
  • Child - Free (under 12 with paying adult)
  • Monthly Pass - TB$65 adult, TB$45 youth/senior
  • Annual Pass - TB$650 adult, TB$450 youth/senior
  • Low-Income Discount - 50% off with qualification

Accessible Transit

We're committed to providing accessible transportation for all:

  • Low-Floor Buses - All buses wheelchair accessible
  • HandyDART Service - Door-to-door service for eligible passengers
  • Audio Announcements - Stop announcements on all routes
  • Priority Seating - Designated areas for mobility devices
  • Service Animals - Welcome on all transit vehicles
  • Assistance - Drivers trained to provide boarding assistance
