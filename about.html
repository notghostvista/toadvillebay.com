ToadVille Bay

Gabriel M.

Creator & Designer of ToadVille Bay

A Mexican-American web developer and designer from California, bringing a unique neurodivergent perspective to digital design. As an autistic creator, I've channeled my passion for accessibility and user experience into reimagining government services. ToadVille Bay is inspired by a game of the same name, where I've transformed its charming virtual country setting into an experiment in how government websites could be more engaging and inclusive. This project combines my love for gaming with my mission to make public services more accessible and user-friendly for everyone.

About ToadVille Bay

An imaginative government website project showcasing modern web design and user experience principles. This creative exercise explores how public services could be more engaging and user-friendly.
