Startup Grant Program

Financial support for new businesses in ToadVille Bay:

  • Amount - Up to TB$10,000 per business
  • Eligibility - Business operating less than 12 months
  • Use - Equipment, inventory, marketing, or working capital
  • Requirements - Must create at least 2 local jobs within 18 months
  • Application - Business plan and financial projections required
  • Deadline - Applications accepted quarterly

Innovation & Sustainability Grants

Support for businesses developing innovative or environmentally sustainable solutions:

  • Technology Innovation - TB$15,000-TB$25,000 for tech startups
  • Green Business - TB$5,000-TB$15,000 for sustainable practices
  • Social Enterprise - TB$10,000-TB$20,000 for community-focused businesses
  • Research & Development - Up to TB$30,000 for product development
  • Matching Funds - 50% grant, 50% business contribution required
  • Reporting - Quarterly progress reports and impact assessment

Business Expansion Support

Help existing businesses grow and create more local employment:

  • Expansion Loans - Low-interest loans up to TB$50,000
  • Equipment Financing - Lease-to-own programs for machinery
  • Hiring Incentives - TB$2,000 credit per new full-time employee
  • Training Subsidies - 75% reimbursement for employee training costs
  • Export Development - Support for businesses entering new markets
  • Property Tax Relief - Graduated tax breaks for expanding businesses

Mentorship Program

Learn from successful local business leaders:

  • One-on-One Mentoring - Monthly meetings with experienced entrepreneurs
  • Industry Experts - Specialized guidance for specific sectors
  • Peer Networks - Connect with other business owners at similar stages
  • Workshops - Group sessions on common business challenges
  • No Cost - Free program for all ToadVille Bay businesses
  • Flexible Schedule - Virtual and in-person options available
