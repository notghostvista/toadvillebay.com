Province description

Culture

Languages
Amilpan is mainly English speaking, with some Spanish at certain regions within the province, you will mostly see signs in English, but some in Spanish, more throughout the rural areas.
Traditions & holidays
Food is very traditional here, with the national dish "Buffalo Chips" being created here, grilled chicken dunked into Buffalo sauce and served with chips as a side. Amilpan is also home to the biggest produce of the entire federation.

Geography

Landscape
Amilpan is covered in plains, with a bunch of farms growing crops and breeding animals to make the best meals you will ever have.
