Tepayán

Overview: Tepayán is a coastal and highland province on the eastern side of ToadVille Sur, featuring a mix of urban centres and highland areas. The province supports fishing, small-scale manufacturing, and coastal tourism.

Location: West

Time zone: Mountain Time Zone

Demographics

The province includes a mix of towns and coastal communities with diverse populations. Urban growth has centred around port towns and service hubs.

History

Eastern ToadVille Sur developed around coastal trade and fishing settlements. Over time, port towns expanded and infrastructure improved, linking the province closely with national trade routes.

  • Early coastal settlements focused on fishing and maritime trade.
  • Ports grew during industrial expansion and connected the province to inland markets.

Government & services

Provincial administration manages coastal resources, fisheries regulation, and port infrastructure. Services are focused on supporting maritime industries and community resilience.
